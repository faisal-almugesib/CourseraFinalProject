Our mission

Travel Recommender helps you discover destinations worth the trip — from quiet temples to lively beaches and buzzing cities. Search by mood or place, glance at the local time before you call, and keep a shortlist of everywhere you'd love to go.

It began as a front-end capstone project and grew into a small, polished showcase of vanilla HTML, CSS and JavaScript — responsive, themeable, and built without a framework.
